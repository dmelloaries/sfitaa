Frequently asked Questions

Why should I join SFITAA?

Building a network of other graduates increases your connections. Career opportunities and networking events are two of the biggest reasons to join SFITAA. It is one of the committees that has conducted interesting webinars throughout the global pandemic for the overall development of students.

What does the Alumni Association do?

SFITAA is dedicated towards strengthening the links between SFIT and its alumni with the objectives to unite all alumni of St. Francis Institute of Technology (SFIT) on a single platform of Alumni Association and to encourage fellowship and greater interaction among SFIT alumni in India and across the world. The committee also organises career counselling, seminars, webinars, and networking events which may provide helpful information about the job market to the current students and the Alumni of St. Francis Institute of Technology.
